The Technology Behind the Vision

This project is built on a powerful AI technique called **Transfer Learning**. We utilize the VGG16 model, a deep Convolutional Neural Network pre-trained on millions of diverse images. This gives our model a foundational understanding of visual patterns like edges, textures, and shapes.

We then fine-tune this expert model on our specific dataset of casting products. This final training step teaches it to apply its vast knowledge to the precise task of identifying manufacturing defects. The result is a highly accurate and efficient inspection system developed in a fraction of the time it would take to train a model from scratch.
